How TextbookSpace provides useful features for NotesMafia Contributors

Introduction

TextbookSpace was really a lucky accident. But now that it exists, I have realized how good it could be to make the NotesMafia Contributor's life as easy as possible. From this point, I will be designing features for TextbookSpace with contributors in mind. But before announcing any new features, I would like to take a moment to highlight the convenience of TextbookSpace for the use case of a NotesMafia Contributor.

So let's get right into it!

Ways we benefit NotesMafia Contributors

TextbookSpace provides easy access to PDF copies of the textbooks that NotesMafia Contributors use to make their notes without the need for a Google search. This is it's core feature, and in future updates, we will design this feature to suit the interests of our contributors.

So to further improve the lives of our contributors, let's announce some new features, shall we?

Introducing...

Bounty Boards!

Bounty Boards show items that are urgently needed to be contributed, as well as useful liks to all of the materials needed to complete the bounty, all from TextbookSpace.

This feature is still in development, and more wil be revealed later. For now, live with the suspense. Check back at the TextbookSpace homepage for updates.
